# Initial Concept`n`nWhat do you want to build?`n`nReal estate platform (hybrid web app) focused on selling listings.
# Product Guide - CIMA (Real Estate Platform)`n`n## Vision`nCIMA is a specialized local real estate management platform designed to be the premier intermediary between potential buyers and the architects of 4Cima. It showcases exclusive, architect-designed listings, emphasizing local market expertise and high-quality design.`n`n## Target Users`n- **Potential Buyers:** Individuals searching for locally available premium residential or commercial properties for sale.`n- **4Cima Architects:** The internal team of architects managing their portfolio of properties, tracking performance, and publishing new listings directly.`n- **System Administrators:** Global management of the platform infrastructure.`n`n## Core Features (Public)`n- **Fluid User Experience:** A responsive, interactive interface for property discovery and navigation.`n- **Advanced Property Search:** High-granularity filtering system including property type, price range, location, and specific features (bedrooms, bathrooms, area).`n- **Property Detail Experience:** Immersive viewing with high-quality image galleries, technical specifications, and architectural descriptions.`n- **Direct Lead Generation:** Integrated contact options allowing buyers to initiate inquiries via email or direct links.`n- **Resilient Interface:** Robust error handling ensures a seamless experience even during network interruptions or unexpected failures, with user-friendly recovery options.`n`n## Core Features (Admin - Architects)`n- **Inventory Management:** Full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) capabilities for listings, including draft/publish status control.`n- **Performance Insights:** Basic statistics and metrics to track how listings are performing on the platform.`n- **Enhanced Reliability:** Data integrity is protected during operations with automatic error recovery, session validation, and clear feedback mechanisms for all actions.`n`n## Architecture`n- **Framework:** .NET 9 Blazor Web Application.`n- **Hybrid Approach:** Leverages the unified Blazor hosting model to deliver the interactivity of a SPA for public-facing components while utilizing the security and direct resource access of Server-Side Rendering (SSR) for the administrative backend. This ensures optimal performance, SEO, and security across the entire platform.`n`n## Scope & Constraints`n- **Focus:** Sale listings are the primary focus for the current phase.`n- **Lead Handling:** While the platform generates leads, the actual communication and management of these leads are handled externally (e.g., via email).
